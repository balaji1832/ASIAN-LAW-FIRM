Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Tax notices may include Income Tax notices, GST notices, scrutiny notices, demand notices, compliance-related communications, and departmental queries. Legal and taxation assistance may be provided for reviewing notices and preparing suitable replies. Each matter depends on the records and facts involved.

Professional assistance may be provided relating to refund processing, delayed refunds, refund rectification matters, and preparation of supporting tax documents. Proper review of tax records and procedural requirements may help clients manage refund-related issues effectively.

GST Refund Assistance may include support relating to refund applications, export-related refunds, input tax credit refund matters, refund documentation, and procedural compliance. Proper documentation and review may help in handling refund claims before the concerned authorities.
